Abby Deiter

At a young age, I found that I had a huge desire to create and I immersed myself completely in art. Whether it be drawing on my homework, restaurant menus, or in the sand at my T-ball games (I didn't have much interest in sports), I was always filling in the empty spaces. The passion is still with me today as a young adult. I love experimenting, discovering, and practicing all forms of art: I am a painter, a tattoo designer, a seamstress, and a creator. I am Abby Deiter.
